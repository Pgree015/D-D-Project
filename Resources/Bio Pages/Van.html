Vanendyn "Van" Faefaren

RACE: Half-Elf

CLASS: Paladin 7; Warlock 3

BACKGROUND: Infantry Soldier

SKILLS: Athletics, Insight, Intimidation, Perception, Persuasion, Religion

KEY EQUIPMENT: Greatsword (Valediction), +1 Plate Armor, Animated Shield (Platinum Bulwark)

VAN'S STORY

Van is a former soldier turned adventurer. He is a paladin sworn to serve the greater god Torm, and a warlock of Bahamut. He joined the Order of the Paladins of Torm after losing his lover Aeceran, a fellow soldier, in battle. During his travels, he swore to stop the rise of Tiamat in exchange for power from Bahamut, a sworn vassal of Torm. He travels with his sisters and close friends as he works to stymie the Cult of the Dragon at every oportunity.
