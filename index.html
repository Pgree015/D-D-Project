Absolute Unit: A Site to Show Off D&D Characters!

Welcome to my page!

My name is Patrick and I love to play Dungeons & Dragons. I had my first taste of D&D with a brief 3.5e one-shot and I played a couple sessions of 4e, but it wasn't until several years after 5e came out that I got to play D&D with any regularity. I have now been playing consistently with the same group for about three years.

One of my favorite aspects of the game is developing characters to role-play. My group doesn't have a lot of time to spend on prep, so we play a lot of pre-made modules and take turns at being the DM. Because of that, I'm not only running my own campaign, my friends are also running three other campaigns among themselves right now!

I've created many characters over my time playing D&D, but I have three favorite characters for the campaigns my current group runs:

  • Rysstyl Amakiir: A wood elf ranger, misanthrope, and drunkard.
  • Vanendyn Faefaren: A half-elf paladin, family-man, and soldier.
  • Zane Aldridge: A human fighter, lothario, and racketeer.

I could talk about the stories I've come up with for these characters all day. Of course, that's not what's supposed to be happening around the table, so I will be taking care of that here! I may put some of my friend's characters up here at some point too, as well (if they're brave/industrious enough). Click on one of the portraits below to learn about a Character!
